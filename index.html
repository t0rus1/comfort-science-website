Comfort-Science (formerly known as The Bean Bag) is based in Somerset West, near Cape Town, but we ship our beanbags for FREE to any address in South Africa.

We have hundreds of satisfied clients and have sold well over a thousand beanbags in the last 3 years alone. Not only do the beanbags look great, but more importantly: they feel great!

Choose from 4 sizes (Family, Adult, Gamer and Footbag) and various fabrics (Drama by Hertex, Denim, Corduroy and others we promote from time to time).

The bags consist of an inner bag filled with chipped (flexible polyurethane) foam and an outer washable cover of your choice which zips off and on.

Order online, or contact us if you have special requirements. We make to order and usually dispatch within a week.
