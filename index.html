The Bean Bag is based in Somerset West, near Cape Town, but we ship our beanbags for FREE to any address in South Africa. Choose from 4 sizes (Family, Adult, Gamer and Footbag) and various fabrics (Denim, Corduroy, Drama, Vinyl & Cotton prints).

The bags consist of an inner bag filled with chipped (flexible polyurethane) foam and an outer washable cover of your choice which zips off and on.

Order online, or contact us if you have special requirements. We make to order but usually dispatch within a week.

ADULT SIZES

The adult size is 120 x 120 x 75 (width x depth x height in cm)
